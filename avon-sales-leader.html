Build a Team earn commission

As an AVON Sales Leader you build your own team and watch your earnings grow even faster. You will still earn up to 25% on your personal sales as a representative, plus up to 12% on your team sales in commission

‹ ›

Did you know?

Building a team can offer much higher financial rewards than being an AVON representative. Every campaign (approximately 3 weeks) you will earn commission on your team. The harder you work, the more you will earn, making this ideal for ambitious people.

How to become a sales leader

  • Become an AVON representative first, to learn how AVON works.
  • Ask your sales leader about becoming a sales leader yourself.
  • Prospect for leads, or buy leads from your upline team
  • Sign people up in your team
  • Once you have 5 team members, you will be a sales leader and earn regular commission

The benefits

  • Earn up to 12% on your team sales
  • Earn bonuses for good team performance
  • No limit to your earning potential
  • Ongoing support and training
  • Top performers will qualify for AVON prizes and holidays.
