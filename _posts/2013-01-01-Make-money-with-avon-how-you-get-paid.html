One of the most important things we tell our new Avon representatives is that Avon is not a salary job! The money you earn comes directly from your customers; it is the profit left over after you pay your Avon bill.

Since managing your Avon money is your responsibility, it's important to understand the process of how (and when) you can pay yourself. The whole process can be summed up in the following steps:

Hand out your Avon brochures and order forms
Collect your orders
Submit your order to Avon online (you order products on credit and get a 20-25% discount)
Deliver the products to your customers and Collect the money
Pay your Avon bill and Pocket any leftover profit
