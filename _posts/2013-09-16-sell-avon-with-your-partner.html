There are lots of advantages in selling Avon with your partner. As a representative, there are times when having an extra set of hands can be really useful. As a sales leader, having your partner in your team helps you hit Avon's sales leader targets and has big financial advantages.

For representatives

  • Extra moral support - A little extra support can stop you getting demoralised and losing focus, especially at the start when things are a little tough.
  • Another 'set of hands' - There will always be times when your customers need their order but you have other commitments. If your partner wants to
  • Help on delivery day - With one person calling out orders and the other person packing them into bags, delivery day is a lot less hastle!

For sales leaders

  • Earn maximum commission - With your partner in the first-line of your team, you will take advantage of Avon's network marketing business model and earn the highest possible rate of commission on them.
  • A 'controllable element' - Being able to strategically sign up representatives in two teams allows you to hit Avon's team targets.
