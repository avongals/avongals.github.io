The number one tip for success with Avon (like any direct selling company) is to tell everyone you're selling AVON!

When we say everyone, we mean everyone. If you want to see your sales improve, then you should prepared to venture outside of your comfort zone a little. Before long, you'll find that your confidence is brimming and speaking to complete strangers about AVON is perfectly within your comfort zone. Sounds great doesn't it?

If you're already wondering what people will say to you, that's only natural. Most people are scared of rejection when they first start, but believe me when I say this....the reactions you get from people are NEVER as bad as you imagine! The worst thing that might happen is that they say no!

In fact, that's exactly what you should be aiming for. The best attitude to have with AVON is "GO for NO!" If you're not getting any "no's" then you're not trying hard enough ;-)
