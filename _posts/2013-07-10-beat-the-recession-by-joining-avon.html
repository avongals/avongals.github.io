Caption

With unemployment on the rise again, many people are struggling to find a job. However becoming an Avon representative can offer the perfect opportunity to make extra money. In fact, Avon is about as recession-proof as it gets! Here's why:

  • Constant demand - Every girl wants makeup and needs everyday products such as showergel and room freshener, so you will never be short of someone to sell to!
  • Trusted brand - Avon is one of the UK's most well known and trusted brands. Believe it or not, Avon has actually been going for over 100 years!

Still need convincing?

Express.co.uk recently wrote a fine article about Avon leading the direct sales surge during the recession.

They reported that, according to the direct selling association (DSA), Avon, Amway and other direct selling beauty firms saw sales grow 5 percent to £430 million last year, with Avon being the largest direct-to-customer retailer in the UK. They say that the number of Avon reps has also grown during the recession, due to it's "anyone can join" business model. So there really has never been a better time to join Avon!
