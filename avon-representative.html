Sell AVON make extra income

As an AVON representative you sell AVON products to friends, family and anyone else! It's your business and you will earn up to 25% on everything you sell.

How to become a representative

  • Browse this website to learn about making money with AVON
  • Register to join AVON using the simple signup form
  • Wait for a sales leader to contact you (within 24 hours)
  • Arrange a meeting with your sales leader
  • After the meeting you will officially be an AVON representative!

The benefits

  • Earn up to 25% on your personal sales
  • Get special discounts on AVON products
  • Get free postage on AVON products
  • Meet new people
  • Enjoy running your own business
