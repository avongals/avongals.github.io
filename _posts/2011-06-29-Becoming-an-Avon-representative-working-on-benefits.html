If you decide to run a loyalty scheme for your customers, it's important to think carefully about the value of the gifts you give away.

The official Avon loyalty cards recommend that your customers order from you six times, with a minimum spend of £10, before they get a free gift. You might decide to do something totally different, but we think the Avon loyalty scheme is a great place to start.

So how does a loyalty scheme cut into your profit margin? Well, firstly lets look at how much profit you will make from your 6 minimum customer orders:

2 £10 £2 £2.5

TOTAL £60 £12 £15
