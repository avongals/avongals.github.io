Our Team

Here are some of our team, who have enjoyed great success with AVON. The most hardworking have been able to quite their full time jobs and fulfil their life's ambitions; others have simply been able to enjoy extra income towards everyday living cost or luxuries.

  • Wayne and Nadine Rowlands

    Senior Executive Sales Leaders

    Nadine and Wayne Rowlands joined AVON as Sales Leaders in August 2005, working around their full-time jobs. Wayne worked as an Engineer and Nadine was a Legal Secretary. They started AVON because they desperately needed to earn some extra money...and so their AVON journey began.

    After 3 years Wayne left his full-time job and 2 years later Nadine left her full-time job and since January 2011 they have worked together building their AVON business and team of representatives

    They are both currently at Senior Executive level and regularly qualify for incentives which AVON run, including trips to Prague, Majorca, Monte Carlo, Berlin, Malta and Rome.

  • Vilina Teasdale

    Advanced Sales Leader

    Vilina decided to start AVON when she couldn't find work as a teacher. She wanted to make extra money for herself and her family and soon became a successful AVON Sales Leader.

    Her first goal was to save enough money to go on a trip to New York. Last year, she fullfilled her dream and went on a two week holiday there. Now she is saving for her next life goal!

  • Ashleigh and Paul (that's us!)

    Sales Leaders

    We couldn't talk about success stories without mentioning ourselves. We started selling and recruiting for AVON to save for our wedding. In a short space of time we have built one of the fastest growing teams in the North East. There's no stopping us now.

    As AVON sales leaders we get to meet lots of great people every day. What's more, we get to spend lots of time together, working towards our shared dream!
