Build a Team earn commission

As an AVON Sales Leader you build your own team and watch your earnings grow even faster. You will still earn up to 25% on your personal sales as a representative, plus up to 12% on your team sales in commission

Did you know? Sales Leadership is the network marketing side of AVON.

Building a team can offer much higher financial rewards than being an AVON representative and is highly rewarding in other ways too. Every campaign (3 weeks) you will earn commission on your team.

The harder you work and higher you climb, the more you will earn, making this ideal for ambitious individuals

How to become a sales leader

  • Join as an AVON representative first, to get to grips with how selling AVON works.
  • Ask your sales leader or area manager to teach you about becoming a sales leader
  • Prospect for leads yourself, or buy these from your upline team
  • Sign people up in your team
  • Once you have 5 people in your team, you will be a sales leader and earn regular commission

The benefits

  • Earn up to 12% on your team sales
  • Earn bonuses for good team performance
  • No limit on earning potential
  • Get ongoing support and training
  • Top performers will also qualify for AVON prizes, holidays and publicity.
