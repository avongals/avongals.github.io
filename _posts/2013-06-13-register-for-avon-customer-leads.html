If you want to find new Avon customers in your area, then I would highly recommend that you register on Avon's website for automatic leads. This is an easy way to find new customers WITHOUT having to do any prospecting yourself.

These people have browsed to the Avon website to find a representative in their area, so you know they are keen to become a customer. In our experience these leads can become excellent customers, although we personally find that the orders are more likely to be 'one-off' but with a higher order value than our regular customer orders. Typically we get around 1 new lead per campaign

Avon does not automatically register you for automatic leads, but your sales leader might have done this for you.


To register for automatic customer leads

  • Browse to http://avon.uk.com
  • Log into your Avon account
  • Browse to 'My Profile'
  • Select Preferences
  • Tick the box that says "I would like to receive Customer Leads from Avon and appear in the Find a Representative search results"
